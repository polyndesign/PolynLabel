    Over the years, Dance music has changed and evolved from the infectious grooves of disco to the catchiness of house to the experimentation of EDM and many more!




    Dance-a-rific was founded in 2024 as a way to showcase and highlight all that the genre has to offer. We are a label not only strives in finding new talents but also showcasing talents who are able to express themselves however they like. We accept any artist from any sub-genre of dance music-the possibilities are endless. We serve to foster your creativity and network your art to as many people as possible. Whether you are a fan of dance, an artist, or just want to stop by, come join the Home of the Beat!
